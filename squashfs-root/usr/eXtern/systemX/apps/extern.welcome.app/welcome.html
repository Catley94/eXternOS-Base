Privacy and Personal information

eXtern OS may transmit anonymous data to either extern OS or to third parties in order to enhance your experience. Here is the list of when and why eXtern OS may transmit.data:

Web

I.T.A.I

eXtern OS comes with an artificial intelligence system currently used as a personal assistant mostly from "What would you like to do?" input area. The AI processes a response server side from our servers. Your communications are sent through a secure connection. Your communications will be anonymous, however, we have the ability to view the interactions, but will not be linked to you. With all that said, avoid by all means necessary from providing any information you would like private to the assistant.
